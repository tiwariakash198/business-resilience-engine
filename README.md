# ***Business Resilience Engine***

*An interactive Decision Support System (DSS) for supply chain auditors and operations managers.*

## ***Architecture & Vision***
This engine is being developed using a modular microservices approach to isolate logistical variables from financial burn rates and global macro-events.

*   **Phase 1 (Active Development):** Module 1 (The Supply Shock Logistics Matrix).
*   **Phase 2 (Planned):** Module 2 (Financial Burn & Liquidity Tracker).
*   **Phase 3 (Planned):** Module 3 (Cross-Contamination Routing).
*   **Phase 4 (Planned):** Module 4 (Geopolitical Risk & Macro-Event Engine).
*   **Phase 5 (Future Expansion):** Module 5 (Real-Time API Integration & Automated Contingency).

## ***Module 1: Supply Shock Logistics Matrix***
Currently in development, this module diagnoses the immediate logistical impact of a sudden, localized supply cutoff. It calculates the exact **Line-Stoppage Gap**—the number of days a factory floor will sit idle—by breaking down alternative lead times into real-world components:
1. Supplier Production Time
2. Route Transit Time
3. Logistical Risk Buffers (Standard Deviation)

By comparing the total resupply timeline against current inventory burn rates, the tool outputs a deterministic timeline for supply chain failure.

## ***Module 4: Geopolitical Risk Engine (Upcoming)***
Future architecture will scale to measure systemic failures caused by macro-events, including:
*   Trade route chaos (e.g., Strait of Hormuz / Red Sea blockades)
*   Currency shocks and tariff implementations
*   International sanctions and localized supply chain disruptions
